FAQ

Common questions

Do you teach private lessons only?

Yes. I work one-on-one and with small groups in private settings—often at a client’s home or place of business—so we can focus on your goals and schedule.

What geographic area do you cover?

Southern California broadly—from San Ysidro north toward Marina Del Rey, and inland toward areas such as Calexico, Diamond Bar, and Palm Springs, depending on the project.

I’m new to martial arts. Is that OK?

Absolutely. Programs are built around your experience level, whether you are starting from zero or returning after time away.

Do you work with law enforcement or agencies?

I have extensive experience with defensive tactics and related topics for agencies worldwide. Use the contact section to describe your organization’s needs and we can discuss scope and scheduling.

How do I get started?

Email roy@royharris.com or call 98-98-Harris. Briefly describe what you want to achieve, and we’ll go from there.
