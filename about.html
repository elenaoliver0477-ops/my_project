About Me

I began my martial art journey back in January of 1981. Since then, I've had the opportunity to train in twenty-seven (27) different styles of martial art under fifty-one (51) instructors. As a result of my study of these different styles, I have achieved the following ranks and certifications:

  1. Certified California Peace Officer Standards and Training (P.O.S.T.) Defensive Tactics Instructor
  2. California P.O.S.T. Certified Academy Instructor (AICC)
  3. Seventh-Degree Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu under Grandmaster Joe Moreira
  4. Basic Level Instructorship in Kalis Ilustrisimo under Punong Guro Tony Diego and Guro Romeo Macapagal
  5. Green Glove in Boxe Francaise Savate under Professor Salem Assli
  6. Senior Full Instructorship in Jeet Kune Do Concepts / Filipino Martial Arts under Sifu Paul Vunak (former)
  7. Associate Level Instructorship in Jun Fan Gung Fu under Guro Dan Inosanto (former)
  8. Associate Level Instructorship in Filipino Martial Arts under Guro Dan Inosanto (former)
  9. Apprentice Level Four Instructorship in Jun Fan Gung Fu under Sifu Larry Hartsell (former)
  10. HKC (Hardstyle Kettlebell Certification) Certified Instructor from Comrade Pavel Tsatsouline.
  11. Honorary Fourth Degree Black Belt in Taihojutsu (Police Tactics) under Sensei Tom Snowden.
  12. Honorary Third-Degree Black Belt in Sho Shin Ti modes Karate under Sensei Larry Kurtz.
  13. Certified Adjunct Instructor for Safe Restraints, Inc. (The Wrap)

Since 1987, I have taught Defensive Tactics (i.e. Edged Weapon Defenses, Ground Control Tactics, Arrest and Control Tactics, and Functional Fitness) to numerous law enforcement agencies as well as combatives to various military groups around the world. Featured

I was featured on a syndicated television show entitled "Don't Be A Target: A Television Guide to Crime Prevention". I also appeared on a local San Diego based news station because of my expertise in Jeet Kune Do.

I was featured in Black Belt Magazine, several Southern California newspapers, as well as a handful of international martial arts magazines.

Back in the mid-1990's, I was interviewed by a San Diego based radio station and even answered questions as callers called into the station to learn about martial arts and self-defense. Because of the 100+ self-defense workshops I taught to the general public, free of charge, I was nominated as "Angel of the Week" by this radio station.

Additionally, I have been featured on numerous podcasts. I will list some of them on my Resources Page.
